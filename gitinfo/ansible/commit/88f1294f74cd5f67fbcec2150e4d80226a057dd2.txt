+.. _ini_file:
+
+ini_file
+````````
+
+.. versionadded:: 0.9
+
+
+Manage (add, remove, change) individual settings in an INI-style file without having to manage the file as a whole with, say, ``template`` or ``assemble``. Adds missing sections if they don't exist. 
+
+
