    CLN: Use timezone natively supported by pytz
    
    Apparently Asia/Beijing is actually used in China, but it's
    not natively supported in timezone software. See for example:
    https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/libgweather/+bug/228554
    
    That said, example timezone in docs should at least work, so
    that's the reason for the change
