    fix(events): Correct event time tooltip sizing (#11358)
    
    The tooltip was partially hiding the date time within it, this inserts
    the tooltip to it's parent container instead of at the body level.
    
    This is how the tooltip used to render before
    67ad02531ac4b6d4fd02274565e37f387edd01b7 was applied, causing all
    tooltips to render on the body.
    
    I don't know exactly why having the tooltip within the parent container
    fixes the overflow, but this is somewhat of a bandaid until we can
    improve tooltips.
