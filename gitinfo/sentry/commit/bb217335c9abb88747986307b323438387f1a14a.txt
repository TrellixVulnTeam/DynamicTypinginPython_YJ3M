    fix: Ensure the `fetchData` method uses the right value of `this`
    
    When the `this.fetchData` methods are invoked as the `onError` or
    `onRetry` callbacks below, they will be invoked as function calls
    where `this` is not an instance of `ProjectPluginsContainer` or
    `GroupUserFeedback`, causing the subsequent property accesses on
    `this` to behave unexpectedly. Rewriting the method definitions to be
    of the same arrow form as `handleChange` in `projectPlugins/index.jsx`
    fixes this problem.
