+~~~~~~~~~~~~~
+

+enabled detection within the reverse proxy (see the instructions above), as
+well as within the Sentry configuration:
+
+::
+
+    SECURE_PROXY_SSL_HEADER = ('HTTP_X_FORWARDED_PROTO', 'https')
