    Remove `-f` shortcut for `--logformat`
    
    This was brought up in https://github.com/getsentry/sentry/pull/3685,
    but not addressed.
    
    We shouldn't provide such a rarely needed option a nice `-f` shortcut
    which is commonly used for `--force` and would be expected as such. This
    also conflicts with current uses of `-f` on other commands.
