    Fix invalid URL in admin/projects/list.html.
    
    If the project_list contains a project that is not associated with a
    team (such as "Sentry (Internal)"), then URL generation for the
    sentry-stream and sentry-manage-project URLs breaks.  The "fix" is to
    not emit the URLs that we cannot possibly generate without a team slug.
