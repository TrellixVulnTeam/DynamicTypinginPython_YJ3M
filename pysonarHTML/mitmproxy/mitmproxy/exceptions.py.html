   1 """
   2 We try to be very hygienic regarding the exceptions we throw:
   3 
   4 - Every exception that might be externally visible to users shall be a subclass
   5   of MitmproxyException.p
   6 - Every exception in the base net module shall be a subclass
   7   of NetlibException, and will not be propagated directly to users.
   8 
   9 See also: http://lucumr.pocoo.org/2014/10/16/on-error-handling/
  10 """
  11 
  12 
  13 class MitmproxyException(Exception):
  14 
  15     """
  16     Base class for all exceptions thrown by mitmproxy.
  17     """
  18 
  19     def __init__(self, message=None):
  20         super().__init__(message)
  21
