   1 """
   2 Modify a streamed response.
   3 
   4 Generally speaking, we recommend *not* to stream messages you need to modify.
   5 Modifying streamed responses is tricky and brittle:
   6     - If the transfer encoding isn't chunked, you cannot simply change the content length.
   7     - If you want to replace all occurrences of "foobar", make sure to catch the cases
   8       where one chunk ends with [...]foo" and the next starts with "bar[...].
   9 """
  10 
  11 
  12 def modify(chunks):
  13     """
  14     chunks is a generator that can be used to iterate over all chunks.
  15     """
  16     for chunk in chunks:
  17         yield chunk.replace("foo", "bar")
